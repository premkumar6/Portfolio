Project Date

Dec 1st, 2023

Role

Healthcare Data Scientist, Research

HealthCodeAI set out to revolutionize the accuracy of hospital billing predictions, harnessing the power of artificial intelligence to streamline financial workflows in healthcare. By employing natural language processing (NLP) and advanced deep learning models like recurrent neural networks, the project explored new avenues for more accurate billing forecasts. A key part of this endeavor was the integration of BioBERT, which enhanced the classification of complex medical codes across diverse clinical datasets, driving predictive insights that are critical in healthcare billing. Additionally, I took on the responsibility of documenting these findings in comprehensive research papers, creating a bridge of understanding that strengthened connections with stakeholders and encouraged further collaboration on AI-driven solutions. This project not only refined financial precision but also contributed meaningfully to the integration of AI in healthcare billing processes.
