Project Date

November 29, 2022

Role

UX Programmer, 3D Animation

MotionTrack was developed to transform patient care by applying advanced data processing and visualization techniques. By implementing specialized methods to filter artifacts and smooth patient data, the project significantly improved data quality and reliability. Refined statistical and machine learning models accurately captured key clinical metrics like Range of Motion and functional performance, directly supporting treatment planning and patient outcomes. MotionTrack also introduced an innovative avatar-based system that precisely recreated patient movements, offering clinicians an interactive tool to track progress. This visualization breakthrough empowered clinicians to provide targeted support, enhancing patient adherence to treatment plans and helping to reduce recovery times.
