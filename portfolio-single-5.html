Project Date

June 25, 2021

Role

Data Analysis, Real World Data

AudioImpact was a pioneering research initiative designed to reveal the auditory consequences of the COVID-19 pandemic. The project identified a concerning rise in hearing impairment cases among patients, drawing on extensive data from healthcare facilities across the country. To ensure these findings informed healthcare responses, I developed a centralized repository that consolidated critical insights from over 200 studies, offering streamlined access to over 30 stakeholders. Leading a team of five researchers, I oversaw the evaluation and organization of more than 300 scholarly articles, all meticulously reviewed for quality within a tight timeline. This work added valuable knowledge that helped shape healthcare protocols to address COVID-19's auditory impacts effectively.
